What is StockStockr?

StockStockr is a web based application dedicated to providing a stock market investment prediction service that prioritizes usability and transparency. We hope to help people make informed investment decisions weather your a stock trading pro or completely new to the stock market.

More specifically we provide various information about certain stocks:

    - Prediction of closing prices over time
    - Display of helpful numerical data
    - Analysis of popular sentiment toward a given stock
    - Historical data


By creating an account on Stock Stockr, you can save stcoks to your watchlist for easy continued access of StockStockr services. You can also practice trading with a virtual currency counter (VCC) linked to your account. Below are more in-depth looks at all of the features of the Stock Stockr Application.

Prediction of Closing Prices

How do we make predictions of stock prices?

We here at StockStockr use a variety of machine learning algorithms to analyze the historical data of a stock. By analyzing how a stock has done in the past, we can make some assumptions as to how it will preform in the future. While this is not a 100% accurate (nothing ever is) it does help compile many factors and provide a well educated prediction.

Below is a line graph that appends the latest stock data (in black) with predicted data points (in green). This graph can help you visualize the general trend of a stock.



Our goal at StockStockr is to provide benefical stock analysis to individuals who may not have the time to do so on their own. It is not meant to be a magic machine that spits out money.

This is why we combine this method with other forms of analysis so that you can get a better look at the big picture surrounding a given stock.

Numerical Data

What does the numerical data mean?

When it comes to stock trading, you have to look at the numbers. This is why we have provided a few pivotal metrics for each stock that should be kept in mind.

These metics include:

    - Latest Closing Price:
    The last closing price on record, ideally what you would pay for a share of this stock.

    - Predicted Price (1w)
    Our predicted price for a stock at a given date. This is not guarenteed, it is an estimate.

    - Other Metrics
    What we have to say about it.

    - Other Metrics
    What we have to say about it.


These should help you get a ballpark estimate and better understand the risks and rewards of trading a particular stock.

Stock Sentiment

What is a stocks sentiment?

Not every variable that affects a stocks price is a number, and we at StockStockr want to make sure our predictions account for qualitative reasoning. In doing this, we have created an algorithm that anaylizes thousands of online posts and tweets to amass a general attitute about a given stock. We then display this information visually to better help you understand how the world feels about a given companies future.



This can be an invaluable tool in helping you determine is a stock is looking to grow or tank.

How do I request a new stock be trained?

At the moment, we do not have many different stocks available that have been trained and ready to use on the StockStckr app. For now, if you would like us to add another stock, contact us through the "Contact Us" or "Request Training" buttons on the navbar. Watchlists

How do I add astock to my Watchlist?

First, make sure you at the home, browse, or info pages where selectable links to stock data can be found. Once you're there, just click on a stock you would like to add, then click on the dropdown that says "Add *stock* to Watchlist".



From now on, you can find that stocks prediction information on your account page.
